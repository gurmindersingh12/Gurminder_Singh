About

From disease biology to breeding-ready genetic tools.

Research focus

My work connects molecular genetics with practical breeding outcomes. I investigate the genetic architecture of disease resistance and susceptibility, identify key loci and candidate genes, and use genomic and functional tools to support resilient crop development.

I am especially interested in wheat-pathogen interactions, susceptibility gene biology, high-resolution mapping, diagnostic marker development, and functional validation through gene editing and complementation.
