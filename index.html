About

A research profile designed for quick credibility and deeper exploration.

Research focus

My work connects molecular genetics with practical breeding outcomes. I investigate the genetic architecture of disease resistance and susceptibility, identify key loci and candidate genes, and use genomic and functional tools to support the development of resilient crop varieties.

The portfolio is structured to help collaborators, hiring committees, and research partners quickly understand my expertise, outputs, and scientific direction.
